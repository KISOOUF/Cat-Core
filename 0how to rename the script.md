## How to rename Cat-Core?
1. Go to https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KISOOUF/PSX/main/Cat-Core
2. Find  `_G.UITITTLE = "NAME"` and rename it. [ DO NOT REMOVE THE " "]
3. Done, just make a new paste and make it  loadstring.
4. Enjoy!
